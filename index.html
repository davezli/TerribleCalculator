About

Made by Zhi Rui Du and Dave Li in March 2016 for TerribleHacks.

Thanks to mental_floss and motivoso85 for the pictures, MIT for the bootstrap template, and math.js for the math libraries.

Usage

'12 / (2.3 + 0.7)' = 4
'sin(45 deg) ^ 2' = 0.5
'9 / 3 + 2i' = 3 + 2i
'det([-1, 2; 3, 1])' = -7
